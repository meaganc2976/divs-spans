DIVs and SPANs

Similarities

  • they are both containers that hold content
  • they are invisible by default
  • they are styled with <style> and <class>

Differences

  • <div>: block
    • used for grouping elements
  • <span>:inline
    • used to change the style of something on a webpage without affecting the context it is within
